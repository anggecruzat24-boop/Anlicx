Angelica Cruzat

BSED FILIPINO III | Mindoro State University

Hello! I am Angelica Cruzat, coming all the way from Poblacion III, Tamaraw, Victoria, Oriental Mindoro. I am currently a 3rd-year student taking up Bachelor of Secondary Education Major in Filipino at Mindoro State University.

If I’m being honest, my heart was originally set on the skies. My dream was to be a Flight Attendant. However, my parents decided that I should pursue teaching instead. At first, it was really hard for me. It’s difficult to force yourself into something that wasn’t your first choice. But as time went by, I slowly fell in love with it. Now, I can finally see myself standing in front of a classroom, happily sharing knowledge and inspiring my future students.

A few fun facts about me:

  • Coffee Enthusiast: I usually drink five cups of coffee a day!
  • A Beauty Queen’s Journey: Joining pageants helped me find my self-confidence again.
